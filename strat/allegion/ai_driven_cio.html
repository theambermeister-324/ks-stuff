The Risk

AI Without a Control Plane Increases Operational Exposure

Ungoverned AI Output

AI can generate UI and code quickly but without policy enforcement, outputs drift from approved standards.

Impact if unaddressed: 30-50% of AI output requires rework; 2-4 week delivery delays per release.

Tool Sprawl & Drift

Design system assets live across tools and repos, creating conflicting sources of truth and operational inconsistency.

Impact if unaddressed: 15-25% duplicate component build; $200-400K/year in redundant effort.

No Audit Trail

Without a control plane, there is no systematic way to validate, trace, or approve AI actions.

Impact if unaddressed: Compliance reviews add 1-2 weeks; elevated audit findings and risk exposure.

Accumulating Technical Debt

Inconsistent AI output creates rework, compliance risk, and uncontrolled component proliferation.

Impact if unaddressed: 20-30% velocity drag; 2-3x cost to retrofit governance later.
