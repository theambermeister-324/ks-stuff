Knapsack Business Case

Driving Efficiency, Scale, and AI-Readiness for Allegion's 2026 Design System Initiative

Executive Summary

Bottom Line: Knapsack directly addresses Allegion's three critical WIN themes for 2026—Efficiency & Speed, Scalability & Consistency, and AI Readiness—by providing a comprehensive design system platform that unifies 10+ brands, accelerates product development, and future-proofs workflows with AI-driven automation.

While ZeroHeight offers basic documentation capabilities at a lower price point, Knapsack's investment delivers measurable ROI through automated code synchronization, cross-platform support (web + mobile), AI-powered component generation, and enterprise-grade enablement services that ensure rapid time-to-value for a team building its first design system.

Alignment to Allegion's WIN Themes

WIN Theme 1: Efficiency & Speed

Current State Challenge

Allegion is delivering significantly slower than competitors due to lack of reusable components and design guidelines. Each product team reinvents the wheel, building designs from scratch. It took an entire year just to establish basic style guides. Leadership (including the CEO) has mandated "efficiency" as the driving theme for 2026—the design system must enable teams to move faster and do more with less.

Knapsack Solution & Value

Knapsack accelerates delivery by automatically synchronizing code with documentation, eliminating manual updates that slow teams down. Unlike ZeroHeight's static documentation that requires constant maintenance, Knapsack's package introspection and Storybook embedding keep everything current automatically.

  • Automated sync: Code changes instantly reflect in documentation—no manual updates needed
  • Live component library: Developers and designers work from real, production-ready components, not screenshots
  • Eliminate redundant work: Reusable components and clear guidelines prevent teams from rebuilding the same patterns across 300 engineers
  • Self-service documentation: 200+ stakeholders can find answers without bottlenecking the 20-person design team

WIN Theme 2: Scalability & Consistency Across 10+ Brands

Current State Challenge

Fragmented design across acquisitions: ~10 different brands in Allegion's portfolio (many from recent acquisitions) each work in silos with their own styles. This creates brand dilution and prevents scaling design output without proportional headcount increases. The goal is "everyone do it the same" with one source of truth that accommodates different brand themes while maintaining consistency.

Knapsack Solution & Value

Knapsack's multi-system governance architecture is purpose-built for organizations managing multiple brands and product lines—exactly Allegion's situation with 10+ brands and diverse tech stacks.

  • Multi-system support: Manage core design system + brand-specific variations (CIB equivalent) in one platform with permission-based views
  • Design token engine: Centralize base tokens (spacing, typography, core colors) and theme them per brand—exports to CSS/JS/iOS/Android
  • Cross-framework compatibility: Support Angular, React, Material Design, Ionic, and emerging frameworks like ShadCN without rebuilding documentation
  • Single source of truth: One platform serving 12,000 employees across all products, ensuring "everyone does it the same"

WIN Theme 3: AI Readiness & Innovation

Current State Challenge

Future-proofing for AI workflows: Allegion's UX leadership envisions AI reducing design team from 20 to 1-2 people by automating design generation. They're exploring how AI can accelerate workflows in 2026 and need a platform that supports this vision. The design system investment must lay groundwork for AI-enabled, next-generation workflows—not lock them into outdated tooling.

Knapsack Solution & Value

Knapsack is the only enterprise design system platform with production AI capabilities today, positioning Allegion ahead of competitors still working with static documentation tools.

  • MCP (Model Context Protocol) Server: AI agents can read and understand your entire design system—components, tokens, documentation, usage patterns
  • Intelligent Product Engine: Automatically generate pages, documentation, and component recommendations based on design system knowledge
  • AI-driven audits: Identify inconsistencies, unused components, and optimization opportunities across your system
  • Future workflow enablement: When Allegion is ready to implement AI-driven design generation, Knapsack is already architected to support it
  • Competitive advantage: While competitors debate Figma vs. no Figma, Allegion gains 12+ months head start on AI-augmented design operations
