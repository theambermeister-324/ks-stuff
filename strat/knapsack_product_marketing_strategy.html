Messaging Toolkit: What to Say Where

Core Narrative: "Collapse the Design-to-Code Gap"

The Problem

Enterprise product teams are stuck with fragmented, slow, expensive design-to-code workflows. Design files live in Figma. Product data scattered across PLM/PIM/ERP. Engineering rebuilds designs from scratch instead of using components. Result: Quarterly release cycles when competitors ship weekly. 30-50% of engineering capacity wasted on rework. $50M-$500M annually in waste per organization.

The Opportunity

The companies shipping weekly are winning markets. They have a unified "digital thread" connecting design systems, product data, and engineering. Their designers ship in hours, not weeks. Their engineers focus on innovation, not rework. Their brand stays consistent. Their product operations run like clockwork.

The Solution

Knapsack IPE: infrastructure (unified design-product-engineering platform) + expertise (embedded FDE support, organizational change management). We create your digital thread. Design system consolidation. Product data unified. Engineering workflows accelerated. Release cycles collapse from quarterly to weekly.

The Benefit

40-60% faster time-to-market. $3-8M annual savings from operational efficiency. 30-50% engineering capacity freed from rework. 95%+ design-code alignment. Teams focus on strategy, not plumbing. Competitive advantage that compounds every quarter.
