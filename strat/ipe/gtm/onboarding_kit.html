Use these definitions when explaining PCRI to customers, partners, and internally. Consistency matters.

What is Product Context?

Product Context is everything in your design system that AI tools need to understand your organization‚Äîtokens, components, patterns, documentation, and standards.

This framing positions design systems not as component libraries, but as the source of truth that feeds AI. Without governed product context, AI tools generate output that doesn't match your organization's standards‚Äîcreating rework, inconsistency, and compliance risk.

Foundation 20% weight

The product context substrate ‚Äî tokens, components, patterns, and documentation

Why it matters: Foundation is the bedrock of AI-enabled delivery. Without structured product context‚Äîtokens, components, and documentation‚ÄîAI tools have nothing to work with. A weak foundation limits everything downstream.

What Changed

The IPE GTM Operating Model v4.2 introduces PCRI-driven qualification and restructures the deal process. Here's what's different:

Before
  • AI signals captured late (during Discovery)
  • Investment Path determined by negotiation
  • BDR owned qualification alone
  • No structured maturity assessment
  • Pricing/scope negotiated per deal
  • Deal Desk = the whole process
  • Mid-funnel pivots common
After (v4.2)
  • AI signals captured at Pre-Qualification
  • Investment Path prescribed by PCRI score
  • Pre-Sales SE owns path hypothesis
  • PCRI benchmarks maturity archetype
  • Pre-defined deliverables per path
  • Deal Desk = one component (Stage 5)
  • 60% fewer mid-funnel pivots

Key Mindset Shift: We're not "selling a solution" ‚Äî we're diagnosing readiness and prescribing a path. The PCRI score tells us what the customer needs; we execute against that prescription.

J3 Activation Deals: Require tri-party approval (GTM + Delivery + Product) before entering Contracting. This ensures resource availability, product fit, and commercial alignment on transformation deals ($740K+).

Deal Desk Accountability: Deal Desk owns procurement acceleration and time-to-signature. Every day in Contracting without progress increases loss probability. Escalate at Day 14.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions we've anticipated. Can't find your answer? Bring it to PCRI Office Hours.

What if a customer doesn't want to do the PCRI assessment?

The PCRI assessment is positioned as a diagnostic, not an obstacle. Frame it as: "Before we scope a solution, let me understand where you are today so we can recommend the right path." If they refuse any discovery, that's a qualification red flag ‚Äî they may not be ready for a consultative engagement.

For time-constrained prospects, use the "quick PCRI" (5-minute version) with the 3 most critical questions per pillar.

Can we override the prescribed Investment Path?

Yes, but rarely. Overrides require AE + CX Partner approval and must be documented in HubSpot. Valid reasons for override:

  • Strategic account with executive sponsorship pushing for accelerated path
  • Pilot conversion from previous engagement
  • Unusual technical circumstances (e.g., acqui-hire situation)

Invalid reasons: "Customer wants a discount" or "I think they're more mature than the score says." Trust the assessment.

What if SE isn't available for Pre-Qualification?

If AI signals are flagged and no SE is available:

  1. BDR schedules Pre-Qual for when SE is available (don't skip SE involvement)
  2. If urgent, AE can cover SE questions using the PCRI question bank (but flag this as sub-optimal)
  3. Never move to Qualification Handover without AI signal capture if signals were flagged

SE capacity is a constraint we need to manage. If this happens frequently, escalate to leadership.

How does this affect my existing pipeline?

Existing deals in Qualification Handover or later: Continue with current process, but try to retroactively score with PCRI if time permits.

Existing deals in Pre-Qualification or earlier: Apply the new process ‚Äî get SE involved if AI signals are present.

Net new deals: Full new process from day one.

Where is the full IPE GTM Operating Model document?

The complete operating model is in strat/ipe/delivery/deal_desk.html. This includes:

  • Full PCRI pillar details and gating rules
  • Investment Path specifications
  • Complete pipeline stage definitions
  • Deal Desk playbooks (health monitoring, rescue, procurement)
  • Implementation checklist

This onboarding kit is a companion document to help you adopt it ‚Äî not a replacement for the full model.

What's the difference between Deal Desk and the GTM Operating Model?

GTM Operating Model = The entire deal process from PCRI assessment through close (7 sections).

Deal Desk = Section 5 of the operating model, focused on active deal management during Contracting/Procurement. It includes health monitoring, rescue playbooks, and procurement acceleration.

Think of it this way: The operating model is the "how we sell" playbook; Deal Desk is the "how we close stuck deals" component within that playbook.
