Gap Assessment Context

Assessment Date: February 27, 2026

Source: Analysis of IPE V1 Offering Kit against Feb 26 Pricing Jam decisions and V1 launch requirements.

Key Insight: Pricing model changed from "Freemium" to "Platform + Consumption." IPE Tier 0 (Knapsack MCP) is included in platform fee; revenue growth comes from consumption tiers. This fundamentally changes how we quote, bill, and track success.

What's Working

  • Offering Kit structure is solid (GTM, Solutioning, Delivery, Procurement)
  • PCRI framework and investment paths (J1-J4) are well-defined
  • Persona value matrix and use cases are clear
  • WBS and RACI matrices provide delivery clarity
  • SOC 2 Type II and data flow diagrams are ready
  • J1/J2 and PCRI SOW templates are ready

Why This Sprint Matters

The Feb 26 Pricing Jam established a new commercial model. Several items that were "in progress" or "pending" are now critical path blockers because:

  1. Consumption pricing requires usage visibility — Can't bill what you can't measure
  2. Legal docs must cover new data patterns — IPE introduces context ingestion/storage
  3. Sales can't quote without tier boundaries — Wide ranges ($24-48K) aren't quotable
  4. CX role shifts to adoption driver — Adoption = consumption = revenue
!
