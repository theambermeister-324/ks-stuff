Why Now? The Context Gap

The Problem

Everyone is building AI generation tools. No one is building the integration layer that becomes infrastructure. AI tools regenerate solutions because they lack organizational context. Every company manually re-teaches context to each tool.

Our Solution

Knapsack aggregates context from ALL sources via integrations, provides persistent, structured context via MCP, and governs AI access. Any generative tool plugged into Knapsack produces better output. We're infrastructure, not competition.

Why Culture is 25%

Based on Google's Project Aristotle research, psychological safety is the #1 predictor of team effectiveness. Westrum's research shows information flow patterns predict organizational performance. Culture is the foundational enabler‚Äîwithout it, nothing else scales.
