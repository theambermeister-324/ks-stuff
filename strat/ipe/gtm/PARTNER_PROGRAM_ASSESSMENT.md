## Conclusion

The Knapsack Partner Onboarding Guide has a strong foundation with clear value proposition, comprehensive role definitions, and structured onboarding. However, it's missing several critical elements that are standard in leading partnership ecosystems:

1. **Partner journey clarity** - Partners need to see the full path from application to active partner
2. **Partner Success Manager process** - Critical for partner success but underdefined
3. **Deal registration** - Essential for partner engagement but missing
4. **Partner tiers** - Standard in all major programs
5. **Certification program** - Expected by partners for credibility

Implementing these recommendations will align the program with industry standards while maintaining Knapsack's unique assessment-driven approach.
