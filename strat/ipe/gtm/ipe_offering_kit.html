Overage & Expansion Model

Predefined Spend Strategy:

  • Initial Contract: Intentionally undersize at Tier 1 knowing adoption will exceed quota
  • Mid-Contract: Customer hits overage territory as adoption grows
  • Expansion Offer: Waive overages if they sign next year at higher tier (e.g., Tier 2)
  • Outcome: Natural expansion path tied to adoption success, not sales pressure

Example: Contract at $50K (Tier 1) knowing they'll consume $100K. At overage point, offer 2-year at $75K/yr = $150K TCV vs $100K one-time. Customer gets predictability, we get commitment.

Solutioning Playbook

Discovery questions, complexity sizing, WBS templates, and RACI for right-sizing implementations.

Discovery Question Bank

Discovery Goal: Validate PCRI self-assessment, determine deployment tier (D1-D4), and confirm investment path (J1-J4). These questions are used by SE during L2 Pre-Qualification and by CX during scoping.

Foundation Pillar (Weight: 25%)

  • Do you have a documented design system today? Where does it live?
  • How many components are in your system? What % are actively maintained?
  • Is your design system in Knapsack, Storybook, or another tool?
  • Do you have design tokens defined? Are they synced across design/dev?

AI Readiness Pillar (Weight: 20%)

  • What AI coding tools are your teams using today? (Cursor, Copilot, etc.)
  • Are AI tools generating UI/component code? How consistent is the output?
  • Have you tried connecting context sources to AI agents? What was the result?
  • What % of AI-generated code requires rework for brand/system compliance?

Governance Pillar (Weight: 25%)

  • Who owns design system governance? Is there a cross-functional team?
  • Do you have contribution guidelines? How are changes reviewed/approved?
  • What happens when teams deviate from the system? Is it tracked?
  • Do you have audit requirements for AI tool usage? (FSS/Healthcare)

Deployment Tier Signals

  • What are your data residency requirements? Can data leave your environment?
  • Do you require SOC 2 / HIPAA / FedRAMP compliance from vendors?
  • Is VPC deployment or on-premises required? (signals D3/D4)
  • What is your typical procurement timeline for SaaS products?

Procurement FAQ (Pre-Answers)

Question Standard Response
Where is data stored? US (default), EU available. D3+ customers can specify region. All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
Do you have SOC 2? Yes, SOC 2 Type II certified. Report available under NDA upon request.
What data do you collect? Design system content (components, tokens, docs) from connected sources. No PII unless embedded in documentation.
Can we do a pen test? Yes for Enterprise+ customers. Coordinate with security@knapsack.cloud. Standard pen test results available for Enterprise.
What's your DR/BC plan? Multi-region failover, daily backups with 30-day retention, documented incident response. Details in SOC 2 report.
Do you support SSO? Yes, SAML 2.0 and OIDC for Professional+. SCIM provisioning for Enterprise+.
