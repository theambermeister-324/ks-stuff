Objection Handling

Common Objections & Reframes

Use these scripts during discovery, demos, and procurement conversations.

"We're not using AI coding tools yet."

"That's actually a great position to be in — you can set up governance before chaos." 78% of enterprise organizations already use AI coding tools daily (Developer Survey 2026), and adoption is accelerating. Getting your design system AI-ready now means you'll be prepared when it hits your teams.

"We can build our own MCP server."

"You absolutely can — and it'll take 6-12 months and $500K+ to get to where we are on day one." Custom builds cover one source. IPE aggregates across Knapsack, Storybook, and Markdown with conflict surfacing, governance, and audit trails. By 2027, we'll have 15-20 integrations. That's the difference between a project and a platform.

"We already use Zeroheight / Supernova."

"Those are docs tools. We're the governed AI control plane." Think file server vs. Salesforce. They store things; we operate things. They don't include MCP, don't do multi-source ingestion, don't surface conflicts, and can't measure design system ROI. Different category.

"The pricing is too high for J3."

"The question isn't cost — it's what the alternative costs." J3 is phased. Phase 1 Foundation ($400K, 16 weeks) proves value before committing to Phases 2-3. Average payback is ~8 months. Without IPE, you're spending more on AI rework than the entire engagement costs. Use the Business Case Builder to make it tangible.

"We don't want to do the PCRI assessment."

"This 10-minute diagnostic ensures we don't waste your time with a generic pitch." It benchmarks you against 158 enterprises in your industry, identifies gaps, and prescribes the right path. Serious buyers engage with readiness conversations. If 10 minutes is too much, the deal isn't real.

"We need to see this work with our specific AI tools."

"IPE delivers via Model Context Protocol — which is the open standard that Claude, Cursor, Copilot, and every major AI tool supports." We don't pick your AI toolchain; we make whatever tools you choose organization-aware. That's the control plane value.

"Our compliance team will slow this down."

"Actually, compliance teams love us." SOC 2 Type II certified with ISO 27001 in progress (target Q3 2026). We have a procurement FAQ, DPA, and security questionnaire pre-built. Most compliance reviews take <2 weeks. See the Offering Kit compliance section.
