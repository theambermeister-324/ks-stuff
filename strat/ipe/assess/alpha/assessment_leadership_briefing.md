# Intelligent Product Engine Assessment
## Leadership Briefing (1-Page)

### Purpose
Launch the assessment as a lead-generation and market-validation tool that positions Knapsack as the thought leader in AI-driven product delivery.

### What the Assessment Now Delivers
- Industry benchmarks + peer comparison, with visual distance-to-top-quartile readiness.
- Journey framing toward AI-ready delivery (no grades or internal metrics).
- ROI business case tied to the recommended path.
- Evidence capture for enterprise credibility (optional link to governance/system-of-record).

### ICP Fit and Funnel Value (Scenario Summary)
- **J1 Foundation**: Early-stage orgs, high education value, diagnostic-first motion.
- **J2 Acceleration**: Trigger-driven, high intent, short cycle, sprint-ready.
- **J3 Activation**: Core ICP, largest measurable gains, services-led transformation.
- **J4 Intelligence**: Expansion-ready, MCP activation and AI enablement.

### Launch Recommendation
Go live with the current assessment as the default lead-gen and validation entry point.

### Conversion Hooks to Emphasize
- "Distance to AI-ready production" and peer comparison.
- "Next best investment" + ROI ranges.
- "Reassess in 90 days" to support follow-up and progress tracking.

### Success Metrics (First 60 Days)
- Assessment completions by industry and journey.
- Evidence link capture rate (enterprise readiness signal).
- SDR meeting conversion rate by journey (J2/J3 priority).
- ROI calculator click-through rate.

### Risks and Mitigations
- **Risk**: Over-indexing on low-readiness leads.
  - **Mitigation**: SDR routing to prioritize J2/J3/J4.
- **Risk**: Market skepticism of generic assessments.
  - **Mitigation**: Highlight industry benchmarks + evidence link capture.

### Decision Ask
Approve launch as the primary market-validation and lead-gen asset.
