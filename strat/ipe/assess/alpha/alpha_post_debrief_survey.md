## Data Collection Notes

### Required Fields for Analysis:
- Participant ID (link to tracker)
- Submission timestamp
- All quantitative scores (Q1, Q4, Q7, Q10, Q12)

### Key Metrics to Track:
| Metric | Target | Calculation |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Question Clarity | >4.0 avg | Mean of Q1 |
| Archetype Accuracy | >75% | % responding 4-5 on Q4 |
| Report Value | >4.0 avg | Mean of Q7 |
| Debrief Value | >4.0 avg | Mean of Q10 |
| NPS | >50 | (% Promoters) - (% Detractors) |
| Testimonial Rate | >30% | % "Yes" on Q13 |

### Trigger Actions:
- **Q1 < 3:** Flag question clarity issues for product team
- **Q4 < 3:** Review archetype assignment logic
- **Q12 ≤ 6:** Flag as detractor, do not use for marketing
- **Q13 = "Yes":** Queue for testimonial follow-up

## Follow-Up Sequences

### For Promoters (NPS 9-10):
1. Send thank you email within 24 hours
2. If testimonial approved, schedule 15-min testimonial capture call
3. Share early access to public launch

### For Passives (NPS 7-8):
1. Send thank you email
2. Note feedback for product improvements
3. Re-engage at public launch

### For Detractors (NPS 0-6):
1. Send thank you email
2. Director personally reviews feedback
3. Consider 1:1 follow-up to understand concerns
4. Do not use for marketing/case studies
