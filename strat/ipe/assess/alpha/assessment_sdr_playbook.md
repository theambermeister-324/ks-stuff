# Intelligent Product Engine Assessment
## SDR Follow-Up Playbook

### Lead Triage (Top of Funnel)
- **High priority**: J2, J3, J4 + evidence link provided.
- **Medium**: J2/J3 without evidence link, J1 with high scores.
- **Low**: J1 with low scores and no evidence link.

### First Reply (All Leads)
Subject: Your Intelligent Product Engine results

Hi {{FirstName}},
Thanks for completing the assessment. Your results show {{Journey}} on the path to AI-ready delivery and how you compare to peers in {{Industry}}. If helpful, I can walk through the benchmark and ROI summary in a quick call.

Would you like a 15-minute results review this week?

### Call Framework (15 minutes)
1) **Confirm context**: "What prompted the assessment?"
2) **Benchmark recap**: "You are above/below peers in Foundation, AI Context, Org Alignment."
3) **Journey framing**: "Your organization is on the {{Journey}} track toward AI-ready delivery."
4) **ROI snippet**: "Typical ROI range for this path is {{AnnualROI}} annually."
5) **Next step**: "Diagnostic or activation sprint based on your path."

### Journey-Specific Follow-Up
**J1 Foundation**
- Goal: Diagnostic and education.
- Talk track: "Focus on governance baseline; activation comes next."
- CTA: Diagnostic scheduling.

**J2 Acceleration**
- Goal: Fast activation sprint.
- Talk track: "Trigger window matters; compress timeline."
- CTA: Sprint scoping call.

**J3 Activation**
- Goal: Services-led transformation.
- Talk track: "Activation unlocks the largest measurable gains."
- CTA: Diagnostic + activation roadmap.

**J4 Intelligence**
- Goal: MCP activation + expansion.
- Talk track: "You are ready for AI-enabled execution with governed context."
- CTA: MCP activation discovery.

### Evidence Link Follow-Up
If evidence link provided:
- "Thanks for the governance/system-of-record link. We can tailor the diagnostic to your current workflow."

### Disqualification Signals
- No sponsor, no budget, no timeline + low readiness.
- Focus on nurturing and reassessment in 90 days.

### Nurture Track (J1 / Low Readiness)
- Share: "AI execution gap" summary.
- Suggest: re-run in 90 days after governance work.
