üéØ Program Overview

The PSRI Alpha Program is a structured validation initiative to test the Product System Readiness Index assessment with a curated cohort of design system leaders and practitioners before public launch.

üéØ Alpha Process: Guided Delivery Model

Unlike the public release, the alpha program uses a human-in-the-loop approach for validation and calibration:

  1. Participant completes assessment ‚Äî No PDF download available; results submitted for review
  2. Director of Delivery reviews submission ‚Äî Validates scoring, calibrates archetype assignment, prepares personalized insights
  3. Curated PDF generated ‚Äî Director adds contextual notes, industry comparisons, and tailored recommendations
  4. 1:1 debrief call scheduled ‚Äî Director delivers PDF and walks through findings (30 min)
  5. Feedback captured ‚Äî Participant provides input on accuracy and value

Alpha Objectives

  1. Validate question clarity ‚Äî Identify confusing or ambiguous questions based on completion patterns and feedback
  2. Test archetype accuracy ‚Äî Director of Delivery compares PSRI output to their assessment of participant's actual maturity
  3. Calibrate scoring algorithm ‚Äî Adjust pillar weights and thresholds based on real-world validation
  4. Refine industry benchmarks ‚Äî Collect baseline data across industries to power "vs. your industry" comparisons
  5. Optimize conversion funnel ‚Äî Measure drop-off points and time-to-complete to improve UX
  6. Generate early pipeline ‚Äî Identify qualified leads ready for IPE conversations

üí¨ Debrief Call Feedback Questions

Director of Delivery should cover these questions during or immediately after each debrief call:

Required Questions (ask during debrief)

  1. Question clarity: Were any assessment questions confusing or hard to answer? Which ones?
  2. Time perception: Did the assessment feel too long, too short, or about right?
  3. Archetype accuracy: "Based on our discussion, does this archetype match where you see yourselves?" (Director validates)
  4. Report value: How useful is this curated report for your planning? (1-5 scale)

Director Calibration Notes (internal)

  1. Score validation: Does the PSRI score match your assessment of their maturity? If not, why?
  2. Archetype override: Should this participant be reclassified? Document reasoning.
  3. Question issues: Flag any questions where participant's answer didn't match reality.
  4. Industry benchmark: How does this compare to others in their industry?

Follow-up Survey (send post-call)

  1. How valuable was the debrief call? (1-5 scale)
  2. Would you recommend this assessment to a peer? (NPS: 0-10)
  3. Can we quote you in our marketing materials? (Yes / No)
  4. Any other feedback on the assessment or debrief experience?
