Knapsack's IPE uniquely addresses all four layers as an integrated platform, rather than point solutions.

Strategic Insight

The largest opportunity (L3: $30–40B) represents the intelligence layer—where product knowledge becomes actionable. This is where Knapsack's IPE creates the most differentiated value by connecting infrastructure, governance, and AI into a single system of record.

The convergence of AI adoption and design system maturity creates a unique window where enterprises are ready to invest in governed intelligence platforms—not just design tools or AI coding assistants, but the infrastructure that makes AI safe and effective at scale.
