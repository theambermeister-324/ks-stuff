MVP1

MVP2
Enablement & Evolution

Switch off the current CMS safely
Unlock long-term value of Knapsack
Core Question
"Can we replace the existing site?"
"How do teams use, extend, and improve this?"
Content
Migrate existing content as-is
Improve, evolve, and expand content

~90 existing items
Net-new content, refactors, consolidations
Migration Approach
Scripted ingestion + limited manual fixes
No bulk migration; incremental authoring
AI Usage
Context enrichment only (non-authoritative)
AI-assisted workflows, automation, insights
Design Changes
None
Possible restructuring, new patterns
Workflows
Minimal (admin-only)
Contribution, review, governance workflows
Permissions
Basic (read vs edit)
Advanced roles, approval models
Integrations
Figma + 1 implementation (minimum)
Expanded integrations (tokens, MCP, tooling)
Audience
Admins and internal maintainers
Broader Adobe teams + partners
Success Metric
Legacy CMS can be shut off
Knapsack becomes the system of record
Timeline Driver
April CMS switch-off
Business priorities & roadmap
Risk Tolerance
Very low (must be stable)
Higher (iterative improvement)
