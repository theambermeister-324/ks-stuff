Building a Business Case

The next evolution of the GSK design system
Everything we've covered today — usage patterns, readiness assessment, team feedback, and the intelligence layer shipping this month — are the building blocks for a business case. The question isn't whether the design system has value; the data shows it does. The question is: what does the next chapter look like, and how do we make a compelling case for it?
The inputs we're assembling together
Usage
210 active users and cross-functional engagement that shows where the design system is essential — and where there's room to grow.
Readiness
Your PCRI score will benchmark GSK across foundation, AI readiness, governance, delivery, and culture — identifying exactly where to invest.
Feedback
Your team's perspective on what's working, where time is lost, and what success looks like — captured today and synthesized into priorities.
Next steps
1 Complete the PCRI assessment (3–5 participants across design, engineering, and product)
2 Activate Knapsack MCP (included) and evaluate multi-source upgrade path
3 Pilot multi-source intelligence with Storybook + MDX once V1 ships end of March
4 Identify your Storybook and MDX sources to connect as context for AI workflows
5 Define adoption benchmarks and success metrics aligned to leadership goals
6 Validate access for globally distributed teams to ensure full participation
We'll bring the business case to you
Once PCRI results are in and your sources are connected, Amber will deliver a readiness report that combines your usage data, maturity score, team feedback, and the intelligence capabilities now available — packaged as a business case you can take to leadership.
