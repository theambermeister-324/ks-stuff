Presented by Amber Atkins
How we'll measure progress together
The Product Context Readiness Index is a new benchmarking tool Knapsack developed to give every customer partnership a shared language, a quarterly measurement point, and a direct input to the business case. It measures readiness across five pillars — many of which directly touch the themes your team raised around governance, AI adoption, agency enablement, and delivery. Benchmarks draw from 158 enterprises navigating the same challenges.
