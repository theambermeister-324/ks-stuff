Objection Handling

"LinearB improves cycle time"

Cycle time measures PR velocity. Knapsack reduces the work itself by providing production-ready components.

Reframe: 47% faster PRs with inconsistent components = faster inconsistency
"LinearB tracks DORA metrics"

DORA measures process health. Knapsack improves product health—brand consistency, design-code alignment.

Reframe: Process metrics vs. product outcomes—which matters to customers?
"LinearB has AI capabilities"

LinearB's AI reviews code for bugs. Knapsack's AI enables design-to-code generation from your actual design system.

Reframe: Bug detection vs. product generation—which transforms how you build?
"We need both tools"

They're complementary, but serve different priorities. The question is which problem Waters needs to solve now.

Reframe: Which gap is bigger? Which investment delivers more strategic value?
