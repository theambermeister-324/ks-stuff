Executive Summary

We're moving from ad-hoc deal qualification to a structured, PCRI-driven process. The PCRI (Product Context Readiness Index) benchmarks each prospect's maturity and prescribes the right Investment Path — including roadmap, resources, timeline, budget, and business case.

Why now? Enterprise AI buyers expect readiness conversations in the first call. Our old process surfaced AI signals too late, causing mid-funnel pivots and misaligned expectations. The new model fixes this.

IPE — The Product

Intelligent Product Engine

The Knapsack platform that delivers governed product context to AI tools—making Claude, Cursor, and Copilot organization-aware.

Delivers: Connect context, govern AI access, enable AI workflows, measure impact.

The one-liner: "PCRI tells you where you are. IPE takes you where you need to go."

What is Product Context?

Product Context is everything in your design system that AI tools need to understand your organization—tokens, components, patterns, documentation, and standards.

This framing positions design systems not as component libraries, but as the source of truth that feeds AI. Without governed product context, AI tools generate output that doesn't match your organization's standards—creating rework, inconsistency, and compliance risk.

Understand PCRI

The Product Context Readiness Index (PCRI) benchmarks an organization's maturity across five pillars. The scored assessment prescribes the Investment Path — including roadmap, resources, implementation plan, budget, and business case.

Foundation (20%)

The product context substrate — tokens, components, patterns, and documentation. Without structured product context, AI tools have nothing to work with.

Key questions: "What is the current state of your design system?" • "How is your design system governed?" • "What is the quality of your documentation?"

AI Readiness (20%)

How prepared is the organization for AI-powered product development? AI tools amplify whatever context they're given.

Key questions: "How are your teams currently using AI coding tools?" • "What is your API maturity?" • "What is your organization's AI strategy?"

Gated: AI Readiness score is discounted 50% if Foundation is below 40%.

Governance & Adoption (20%)

Sponsorship, budget, change management, and measurement. Even the best design system fails without executive sponsorship.

Key questions: "Is there a clear executive sponsor?" • "Is there committed investment?" • "How do you measure ROI?"

Delivery Workflow (15%)

Design-to-code workflow maturity. Draws on DORA metrics and Team Topologies research.

Key questions: "How fast can you run experiments?" • "Do you have a platform team?" • "How frequently do you deploy?"

Culture & Contribution (25%)

Organizational health, psychological safety, and community. Culture is the #1 predictor of sustainable adoption per Google's Project Aristotle research.

Key questions: "Who can contribute to your design system?" • "Do team members feel safe to propose new ideas?" • "How freely does information flow?"

Deal Desk manages active deals through Contracting and Procurement stages. This includes health monitoring, intervention playbooks, and procurement acceleration.

Primary Accountability: Deal Desk owns procurement acceleration and time-to-signature. Every day a deal sits in Contracting without forward progress increases the probability of loss.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if a customer doesn't want to do the PCRI assessment?

Frame PCRI as a diagnostic, not an obstacle: "Before we scope a solution, let me understand where you are today so we can recommend the right path." If they refuse any discovery, that's a qualification red flag.

For time-constrained prospects, use the "quick PCRI" (5-minute version) with the 3 most critical questions per pillar.

Can we override the prescribed Investment Path?

Yes, but rarely. Overrides require AE + CX Partner approval and must be documented in HubSpot. Valid reasons:

  • Strategic account with executive sponsorship pushing for accelerated path
  • Pilot conversion from previous engagement
  • Unusual technical circumstances

Invalid reasons: "Customer wants a discount" or "I think they're more mature than the score says." Trust the assessment.

What if SE isn't available for Pre-Qualification?

If AI signals are flagged and no SE is available:

  1. BDR schedules Pre-Qual for when SE is available (don't skip SE involvement)
  2. If urgent, AE can cover SE questions using the PCRI question bank (but flag as sub-optimal)
  3. Never move to Qualification Handover without AI signal capture if signals were flagged

SE capacity is a constraint we need to manage. If this happens frequently, escalate to leadership.

How does this affect my existing pipeline?

Existing deals in Qualification Handover or later: Continue with current process, but try to retroactively score with PCRI if time permits.

Existing deals in Pre-Qualification or earlier: Apply the new process — get SE involved if AI signals are present.

Net new deals: Full new process from day one.

What's the difference between Deal Desk and the GTM Operating Model?

GTM Operating Model = The entire deal process from PCRI assessment through close.

Deal Desk = One component of the operating model, focused on active deal management during Contracting/Procurement. It includes health monitoring, rescue playbooks, and procurement acceleration.
